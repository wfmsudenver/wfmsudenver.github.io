Sometimes I really want to sigh...

After all, sighing brings a lot of oxygen to the brain. Plus, there is just a unique bit of satisfaction to be found in the mix of tiredness and preponderance.

Other times, I really like yawning

I don't really understand the difference between a yawn and a sigh emotionally. The emotions expressed seem to be the same, but maybe we define concious "yawns" as sighs as a means of pretending we have control over our tiredness.
